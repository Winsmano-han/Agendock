## üé¨ **POST-PRODUCTION NOTES**

### **Editing Guidelines**
- **Pacing:** Keep energy high, avoid dead time
- **Transitions:** Use smooth cuts and fades
- **Callouts:** Highlight key features with arrows/circles
- **Text Overlays:** Clear, readable, well-timed
- **Color Grading:** Consistent, professional look

### **Export Specifications**
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (4K preferred)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio:** 48kHz, stereo
- **Subtitles:** Include for accessibility

### **Distribution Versions**
- **Full Version:** 4-5 minutes for presentations
- **Short Version:** 60-90 seconds for social media
- **Demo Reel:** 2-3 minutes highlighting key features
- **Technical Deep-dive:** Extended version for developers
